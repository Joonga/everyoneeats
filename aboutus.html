Phone recycle program

Donate an old phone, these phones can be handed to a person in need so they can access the app, recuse e-waste (electronic waste). Most people hold onto their old phones. Relatively new phones are usually passed onto friends or family however older phones that have stopped receiving support for services such as banking and App Stores do not have much value in them anymore. A lot of these phones however can access a browser and this app can be accessed through a browser as it is a PWA so it adds functionality to the app.
